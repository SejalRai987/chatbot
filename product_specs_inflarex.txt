Product: InflarEx Automation Suite

Overview:
InflarEx is a cloud-based RPA (Robotic Process Automation) tool that allows businesses to automate repetitive tasks without coding.

Key Features:
- Drag-and-drop workflow builder
- AI-enhanced data extraction from PDFs and images
- Multi-platform task scheduling
- Built-in integration with over 50 popular SaaS apps (e.g., Slack, Salesforce, Google Sheets)

System Requirements:
- Supported browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Edge
- Minimum internet speed: 10 Mbps
- Recommended memory: 8 GB RAM

Security:
- SOC 2 compliant infrastructure
- End-to-end AES-256 encryption
- Role-based access controls
