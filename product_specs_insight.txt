Product: InsightIQ Analytics Platform

Overview:
InsightIQ is a self-service analytics dashboard designed for business users with no technical background.

Key Capabilities:
- Natural language query support ("Show me sales growth last quarter")
- Automated anomaly detection using ML models
- Custom dashboards with drag-and-drop elements
- Data connectors for PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Google BigQuery

Compliance & Hosting:
- GDPR and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) compliant
- Hosted on AWS Sydney region
- Daily encrypted backups and 99.9% uptime SLA

Pricing:
- Starter: $49/mo — 1 user, 2 dashboards
- Pro: $149/mo — 5 users, 10 dashboards
- Enterprise: Custom
